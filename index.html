Bukan Aku

Bukan Aku is an independent short movie about Irene, a typical college student who experiences a normal life with her usual daily life, so she wants to try new things and meet people from outside her college because Irene's schedule is quite busy. One day Irene tries to download a dating application, this causes her to meet a person named Moris.
